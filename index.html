Who we are?

At SmartDodos we help organisations and consumers utilize data and digital tools effectively in order to contribute to a more sustainable future. SmartDodos is founded by Eelco Boers a seasoned entrepreneur and professional with ample experience with innovative digital and SMART (analytical) solutions. Aart-Jan Schouten a seasoned management consultant and business developer in the area of innovative (IoT) solutions.

We believe that developing in a sustainable way is the key for ensuring a good future for the forthcoming generations. By using technology and data in SMART way we are confident that we do not have to compromise on business and public objectives while achieving a sustainable future. Both the objectives and sustainability result in the best possible customer experience.
